## 🚀 Introduction
PortraitPro for Windows is retouching software built entirely around the human face. It reads a portrait, understands where the skin and features are, and lets you enhance them with AI rather than laborious manual masking. Photographers and hobbyists reach for PortraitPro when they want polished portraits quickly, with the tone and texture of real skin preserved instead of erased.

## 🌟 Highlights

### Face and Skin
- **Automatic detection** — AI maps the face, eyes, lips, and skin so edits target the right areas.
- **Natural smoothing** — Removes blemishes and evens tone while keeping pores and detail intact.
- **Feature refinement** — Fine-tune facial features with careful, slider-based control.

### Light and Style
- **Relighting** — Reshape and rebalance the light across a face to flatter the subject.
- **Makeup and enhancement** — Apply digital makeup and brighten eyes and teeth for a finished look.

## ❓ Common Questions
**Is PortraitPro free?**
PortraitPro is a paid product with a free trial, so you can evaluate its retouching tools before buying. The paid editions are not distributed as a free full copy.

**Which versions of Windows are supported?**
PortraitPro supports modern 64-bit Windows, including Windows 10 and Windows 11. A currently supported, updated release gives the best results.

**How does PortraitPro compare to Portraiture?**
Portraiture is a plugin focused on skin smoothing inside a host editor, while PortraitPro is a broader standalone app covering skin, relighting, makeup, and feature editing with AI. For a plugin-based workflow, the PortraitPro Studio edition adds Photoshop and Lightroom plugin support along with RAW handling, and PortraitPro Body is available separately for natural body reshaping.
